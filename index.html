What people thinks about us

Megan Fox Stayed 18 Nov, 2019

It was very nice hotel with cleanliness. Staff behavior was good and polite. They welcome us very well. Issue was only that Lift was not in working and we were allotted to 3rd floor and amenities articles were in corner of gallery which were giving bad feeling. Breakfast was good and support of the staff was also very nice. Location is not good as per atmosphere, it is very nearby most of the popular places but self location in a narrow street is not good. Overall it was a good experience and could recommend.

Henri Hobert Stayed 15 Dec, 2020

Nice rooms, highly recommended! I was pleasantly surprised, the accommodation was great. When I come back to Vienna, I stay only in this hotel. I cannot give a feedback on the breakfast, because I do not eat in the morning. There is nothing to complain about.

Tom Dalos Stayed 2 Jan, 2019

The hotel apartment was perfect accommodation for a stay in Vienna. The apartment was extremely spacious and clean. Quiet and safe neighborhood.The main reason to stay at this hotel is you can't beat the price.
